Economic Development Website

Content Strategy: One of the most important and challenging part of the website was the content strategy as the purpose is to attract businesses to a economically weak region to boost the economy. Therefore, the content is entirely focused on amenities, benefits facts, numbers and success stories.


Information Architecture: Another challenge was that there is no search option in the website. Therefore, the infomation architecture is made simple so that the information is wasily findable.

Overview


The City of Hamilton, a rust belt city, is centrally located between the Cincinnati and Dayton metropolitan areas in southeast Ohio. The city's economy was gradually declining for last few decades. However, the latest city administration planned to put significant amount of effort to improve the city's economy by using the advantages that it is an important regional center of business, industry, culture, and government. To showcase all these, the city needed a website solely for economic development purpose besides the main city website.


I did the UX research, Information Architecture and worked on the content strategy for this project.




Product: Economic Development Website


Client: City of Hamilton


My Role: UX Researcher, Content Strategist

Maden Technologies Website

There were many design problems with the original website. Some of them are -

The height and width of the website is one fourth of the viewport and it Is positioned at the top middle part of the viewport.

The selected tab looks almost similar to unselected ones. It should be significantly different.

All the images are made of flash. The user has to download and install flash to see the website properly. If they don't have flash, they don't see many parts of the website.

Multiple different types of fonts are used in different purposes. Also some of the font colors fails to maintain contrast.

Font size is too small to read throughout the website.

Redundant text in many tabs.

Overview


Maden Technologies deliver science and technology solutions to it's clients in the US and abroad, and contribute in improving applications in engineering, policy development, environmental technology, geospatial solutions, computing, and cybersecurity.


I led the clients public website redesign and upgrade project. I managed other designers and finalized the graphics, the UI and UX designs. The I developed and implemented the final product.




Product: Public Website


Client: Maden Technologies


My Role: Lead UX Designer/Developer

Quality Control Advisor

Overview


Quality Control Advisor is an end-to-end solution for managing the entire post-funding quality control and remedy process. It has three views Loan File Management, Remedy Management, and Management Reporting. The approved user can get a summary of QC loan reviews, with details on review status. Filter by sample type, funding date or the date the review closed. With Quality Control Advisor, get greater transparency into the status of the review of a loan.


I led the clients Quality Control Advisor tool redesign and upgrade project. I designed the UI and UX for this product and ensured brand standard by implementing the Style Guide.
